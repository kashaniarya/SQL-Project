Objectives include learning to use SELECT, FROM, and WHERE statements, 
applying the WHERE clause for filtering, 
using aggregate functions like count, sum, and average, 
and understanding the GROUP BY statement. 

PostgreSQL is the required technology.

Part 2 builds upon the movie database design from Part 1 and requires correctly created table definitions. 
Nine SQL queries are implemented, each with specific output requirements such as counting movies by genre, 
calculating average ratings per genre, and identifying movies with at least 10 ratings.
